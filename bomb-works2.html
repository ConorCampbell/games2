Plane Bombing Game

  • Select a map area to begin your mission.
  • Use Arrow Up/Down or W/S to move the plane vertically.
  • Drop bombs with Space or by tapping/clicking the game area.
  • Hit targets for points. Misses reduce your score.
  • Zoom the map with Arrow Left/Right or the zoom buttons.
  • Game ends when all targets are passed or hit.

Select Your Target Area

Choose an area to fly over and bomb targets. Longer maps offer more challenges and potential points!
